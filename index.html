I'm Stefan. A behavioural and cultural researcher based in London.

Right now I'm interested in human-AI interaction. If you are doing something similar, could do with my help, or just have questions, feel free to get in touch.

I'm a UX researcher and behavioural scientist by trade. I graduated from the London School of Economics with an MSc in Behavioural Science in 2020. Then I went to work at the UK Nudge Unit in Westminster for a couple of years, running policy experiments for government departments.

After that, I went to work in behavioural and cultural insight for a private agency, where I've run bespoke research on the human side of AI for Big Tech brands.

I’ve also been experimenting here on using AI to build new types of experience. I’ve made games for fun which you can play, and also immersive tools for learning new skills.

I am focused on the relationship between psychology, media, and technology. I rarely think about anything else, to be honest.

You can find my public writing on my substack. Which is also about those things.
